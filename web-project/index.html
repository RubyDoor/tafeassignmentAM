Welcome

Welcome to The Edge of Now, where creativity knows no bounds and boundaries are constantly pushed. Immerse yourself in a world of contemporary masterpieces that challenge traditional notions and captivate the senses. Our curated collections showcase the vibrant and thought-provoking works of both established and emerging local artists, ensuring a dynamic and ever-evolving experience for our visitors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a deeper connection between the artist and the viewer, bridging the gap between the avant-garde and the everyday. From experimental installations to bold sculptures, our exhibitions transcend conventional boundaries and invite you to engage with art in new and exciting ways. Join us as we celebrate the limitless possibilities of creativity and embark on a journey of exploration and self-discovery through the captivating world of modern art.
